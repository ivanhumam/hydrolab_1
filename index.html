Field Observation System for Data Learning, Numerical and Experimental Model

About Our Laboratory

The Hydroinformatic and System Monitoring Laboratory integrates remote sensing, sensor technology, IoT, and drones for real-time observation of water resources and the environment. Field data collected is not only used for monitoring but also validated and integrated into numerical modeling and laboratory experiments. The laboratory serves as a research hub supporting various applications, ranging from water resource management and disaster mitigation to the development of smart monitoring systems.

Our Activities

Field Observation & Monitoring

Using drones, IoT sensors, and environmental instruments to monitor rivers, reservoirs, coasts, and other water resources.

Data Integration & Modeling

Analyzing field data to validate numerical models and laboratory experiments for accurate and reliable insights.

Experimental Studies

Conducting simulations and small-scale experiments to support hydrological and ecological research.

Technology Development

Developing sensors, IoT systems, and real-time monitoring platforms for innovative applications.

Collaborative Research

Supporting joint projects with industry, government, and other research institutions to advance data-driven solutions.
